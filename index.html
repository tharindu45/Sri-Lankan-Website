Welcome to Sri Lankan History

Sri Lanka has a rich and diverse history spanning over 3,000 years. From the ancient kingdoms of Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa to the colonial rule of the Portuguese, Dutch, and British, Sri Lanka has undergone many changes throughout its history.

Despite these changes, Sri Lanka has maintained a unique cultural identity and has made many contributions to the world, including the development of the Sinhalese language and the creation of the world-famous spice, cinnamon.
